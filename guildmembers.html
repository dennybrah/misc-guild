GUILD MASTERS

GUILD MASTERS are the masterminds of this guild, we have built the infrastructure, and are bound to keep it running. We designed this guild to be performance driven, while having a good time. We are here for the guild and overlook everything. Guilds cannont be ran soley by GUILD MASTERS themselves, we will be delegating positions if the member is interested.

OFFICERS

OFFICERS are the GM's right hand guild members, they will assist in managing the guild, or have vast knowledge on mechanics and are here to assist in management.

CLASS OFFICERS

CLASS OFFICERS are experienced, trusted, and understand the mechanics of the class they are playing and managing. CLASS OFFICERS are here to look over their respective class members, making sure they are following their duties. CLASS OFFICERS will also be grading each of their respective memebers which will present the combined data in the PERFORMANCE sheet on this page.
